Mega Man also known in Japan as Rockman is a Japanese sci-fi game franchise owned by Capcom, originally released for the NES/Famicom in 1987. The game series follows Mega Man, a blue robot created by Dr. Light stopping the schemes of the mad scientist Dr. Wily.

Main series has a roster of 11 games, the stand alone Game Boy series (Known in Japan as “Rockman World”), along with other games that continue with the main storyline Mega Man X, Mega Man Zero, Mega Man ZX, and Mega Man Legends. There are also other spin-offs like Mega Man Battle Network and Mega Man Star Force where they just focus on the fiction of the internet instead of robotics.
