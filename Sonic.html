Sonic is a game franchise created by Yuji Naka, Naoto Ohshima, and Hirokazu Yasuhara and owned by Sega. The games mainly follow Sonic, a fast blue anthropomorphic hedgehog who protects the innocent from a evil scientist with various of gadgets.

Sonic’s main genre of games is platforming but had other genre games such as racing, fighting, party, and sports. The franchise also made profit out of animated series, films, and various of merchandising.

Despite with the occasional struggles of this franchise it is one of the bestselling game franchises grossing over $5 billion by 2014 and the genesis games being the representative of 90’s culture.
